Semiconductor Fabrication and Materials Characterization

Although I switched to mechanical engineering three quarters of the way through my sophomore year, I was still interested in the materials side of applied physics so I decided to take a nanofabrication laboratory class for one of my advanced electives. Assignments included applying semiconductor processing techniques like etching and metal deposition to fabricating an LED from scratch, etching microscopic superhero symbols into silicon using an electron microscope, and fabricating simple microfluidic devices by creating molds out of silicon with custom-designed photoresist masks.
